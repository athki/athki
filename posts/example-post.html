Example Custom Post

January 15, 2024

Published on this website

This is an example of a custom post that you can publish directly on your website. You can write about anything you want here - your thoughts, research, ideas, or anything else you'd like to share with your readers.

Why Custom Posts?

Having your own website posts alongside your Substack gives you flexibility. You can:

  • Write about topics that don't fit your Substack theme
  • Share quick thoughts without the pressure of a newsletter
  • Experiment with different writing styles
  • Keep some content exclusive to your personal site

Writing Format

You can use any HTML formatting you want in these posts. This includes:

  • Bold text for emphasis
  • Italic text for subtle emphasis
  • Code snippets for technical content
  • Links to external resources

Getting Started

To create a new post:

  1. Create a new HTML file in the /posts/ directory
  2. Use this template as a starting point
  3. Add your content
  4. Update data/custom-posts.json with the post details
  5. Commit and push to GitHub

This approach gives you complete control over your content while maintaining the clean, simple design of your website.
